Week 1 Notes


Mobile First Design: Works better than Responsive Retrofitting. It is much easier to expand a page and elements on it than it is to shrink it.

Question: How can I as a designer get into a better habit of working on mobile first? Being on the computer, I just start designing for the computer screen.
Reachability Issues on a phone are fascinating to me. How we hold our phones and use them is very important to know to help determine how to design mobile applications and pages.

Forms: Things to look out for:
  • reduce cognitive load - use the least amount of steps as possible
  • Use float labels
  • Autofill when possible
  • Auto advance to the next field
